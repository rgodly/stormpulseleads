Process

From storm to doorstep
in under 24 hours

Every step is automated against verified government data — no manual pulling, no outdated lists.

⚡
Step one

Storm detected

NOAA hail reports and NWS alerts are monitored every 30 minutes. When 1"+ hail hits any DFW county, the pipeline activates automatically.

📍
Step two

Impact zone mapped

GPS coordinates from the storm report define a radius. Every residential parcel within the hail path is identified using county GIS data.

🏠
Step three

Homeowners matched

Each property is cross-referenced against DCAD and TAD records. Owner names, addresses, and property class are verified and scored 0–100.

📋
Step four

List delivered

You receive a clean CSV within 24 hours — sorted by score, filtered to single-family residential only. Ready to work immediately.
